A local-first knowledge base engineered to bridge the gap between Apple Notes and Obsidian. WebNotes operates on a dual Tauri (Rust) and Next.js runtime, featuring a Tiptap rich text editor backed by a Yjs CRDT collaboration engine. By implementing stateless sync on Vercel serverless functions, it ensures seamless, conflict-free document synchronization with graceful degradation to local disk—achieving zero-cost cloud infrastructure at scale and reaching 120k+ views at launch.
